Building a Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App with Flask and Machine Learning

In the age of technology and information, access to accurate and timely healthcare is more critical than ever. With the increasing importance of remote healthcare solutions, we developed a symptom-based disease diagnosis web application using Flask and machine learning. The platform helps users identify possible illnesses based on their symptoms.

The Problem

Many people experience symptoms and want quick answers about their health conditions. However, it can be difficult to differentiate between diseases because many symptoms overlap. Our goal was to create a system where users could input their symptoms and receive possible diagnoses instantly.

The Solution

We built a web application that allows users to enter symptoms they are experiencing. The system then uses a trained Decision Tree Classifier to predict the most likely disease based on those symptoms.

  1. Symptom Input: Users enter symptoms through an easy-to-use interface that supports a wide range of medical symptoms.
  2. Machine Learning Model: A Decision Tree Classifier model was trained using a dataset containing symptoms and corresponding diseases.
  3. Prediction: The model analyzes the symptoms and predicts the most probable disease.
  4. Additional Information: The system also provides extra information such as disease description, precautions, medications, diet suggestions, and workout advice.

Key Features

  • User-Friendly Interface: Simple and intuitive design for easy symptom entry.
  • Accurate Predictions: Machine learning algorithms provide reliable disease predictions.
  • Comprehensive Information: Detailed guidance including precautions, medicines, diet, and workouts.
  • Educational Content: Informative blog posts that help users learn about different health topics.

Conclusion

Our Symptom-Based Disease Diagnosis Web App combines machine learning and healthcare information to provide quick insights into possible illnesses. The system helps users understand their symptoms and make informed decisions about their health. By integrating AI technology with healthcare knowledge, we aim to make medical information more accessible and useful for everyone.
